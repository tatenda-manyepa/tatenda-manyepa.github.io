Enterprise Operational Systems Modernisation

AWS-first cloud transformation and operational modernisation programme for a fictional enterprise fulfilment platform.

Executive Summary

Apex Operational Systems Ltd is a fictional enterprise operating a partially automated order fulfilment environment supporting nationwide automotive parts distribution across the United Kingdom.

The operational platform consists of interconnected applications, databases, messaging services, fulfilment systems, reporting capabilities, and robotic fulfilment technologies. These systems collectively support inventory management, order processing, fulfilment execution, shipping preparation, and operational reporting.

While the current environment supports day-to-day operations, several technical and operational challenges increase business risk, limit scalability, and impact overall service resilience. Key concerns include recurring service outages, manual failover processes, infrastructure capacity constraints, database performance limitations, messaging reliability issues, limited observability, and an immature disaster recovery posture.

Business Drivers

The transformation programme is driven by the need to improve resilience, reduce manual operational effort, strengthen disaster recovery, improve monitoring, and prepare the platform for AI-assisted operational intelligence.

Improve Operational Resilience

Reduce recurring outages and improve service continuity across fulfilment, inventory, and order workflows.

Improve Disaster Recovery

Introduce clearer recovery objectives, validated backups, recovery runbooks, and reduced business continuity risk.

Improve Observability

Move from reactive support to proactive monitoring, alerting, operational dashboards, and faster root cause analysis.

Reduce Manual Operations

Reduce manual failover, service recovery, health checks, and repetitive incident investigation effort.

Enable Scalability

Support operational growth, peak demand, and more flexible infrastructure scaling.

Enable AI Operations

Create the foundation for AI-assisted troubleshooting, knowledge retrieval, RCA support, and intelligent incident response.

Current Challenges

Service Outages

Recurring outages impact inventory allocation, order processing, fulfilment execution, and operational throughput.

Manual Failover

Recovery activities depend heavily on support personnel performing manual recovery and failover actions.

Database Performance

Long-running queries and inefficient processing can affect application responsiveness and fulfilment throughput.

Monitoring Gaps

Limited monitoring capability means issues are often identified after service degradation or failure.

Messaging Reliability

Delayed or blocked message processing can disrupt workflows and increase operational support effort.

Disaster Recovery Risk

Disaster recovery maturity is limited, with recovery activities largely reactive and not consistently tested.

Transformation Objectives

Resilient Platform

Design a more reliable operational platform with improved availability and reduced outage impact.

Cloud Modernisation

Move from legacy on-premises infrastructure toward an AWS-first cloud operating model.

Operational Visibility

Introduce centralised monitoring, dashboards, alerts, logging, and operational intelligence.

Security & Governance

Strengthen access control, secrets management, auditability, operational governance, and compliance readiness.

Automation & CI/CD

Improve change management, validation, release safety, rollback capability, and controlled hotfix deployment.

AI-Assisted Operations

Enable guided troubleshooting, knowledge retrieval, ticket intelligence, RCA support, and incident response acceleration.
